Contas e Letras

Description

Contas e Letras is a private school in Maputo, Mozambique, offering a nurturing and stimulating learning environment for students from preschool to Grade 9. We foster intellectual curiosity and equip our students with the skills they need to thrive in a globalized world.

Our Approach

Bilingual Education

We provide instruction in both Portuguese and English, starting from preschool. This immersion approach allows students to develop fluency and confidence in both languages.

Broad Curriculum

Our curriculum encompasses a wide range of subjects, including:

  • Portuguese
    • Matemática
    • Ciências Naturais
    • Inglês
    • Educação Física
  • English
    • Mathematics
    • Natural Science
    • Maths
Holistic Development Qualified Teachers
We go beyond academics, focusing on the social, emotional, and physical development of each child. Our dedicated and experienced teachers are passionate about creating a positive and engaging learning experience for all students.
